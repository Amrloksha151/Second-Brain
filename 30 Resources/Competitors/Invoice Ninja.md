# Invoice Ninja

## 📖 Definition
An open-source invoicing and billing platform known for its "API-first" architecture and flexible self-hosting options.

## 🛠️ Key Features
- **Open Source:** Full control over data for self-hosted users.
- **Client Portal:** Dashboard for client payment management.
- **Multi-Gateway:** Integrates with 40+ payment gateways.
- **Customization:** HTML/CSS templates for professional branding.
- **Automation:** Recurring billing and auto-billing features.

## 💰 Pricing & Business Model
- **Forever Free:** $0 (Up to 5 clients, Cloud hosted).
- **Ninja Pro:** ~$10/mo (Unlimited clients, Cloud hosted).
- **Self-Hosted:** $0 (Free, requires $30/yr for white-labeling).
- **Enterprise:** ~$14/mo (Advanced reporting, multi-user).

## 💻 Developer Experience
- **API-First:** Every feature is accessible via the RESTful API.
- **Stack:** Built on Laravel (PHP), making it highly extensible.
- **SDKs:** PHP, Flutter/Dart, and Go.
- **Authentication:** `X-API-Token` header.

## 🔗 Connections
- **Projects:** [[BlackBills]]
- **Competitors:** [[Bill.com]], [[Wave]], [[Zoho Invoices]]

## 📝 Notes
- Most developer-friendly competitor due to open-source nature and API-first design.
- Direct competitor to [[BlackBills]] in terms of catering to developers and scaling businesses.
- Self-hosting option is a unique selling point for privacy-conscious businesses.
