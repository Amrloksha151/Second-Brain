# Wave Accounting

## 📖 Definition
A "freemium" financial software suite tailored for freelancers and small businesses, known for its free core invoicing and accounting tools.

## 🛠️ Key Features
- **Truly Free Invoicing:** No limits on invoices or clients.
- **Multi-Currency:** Support for over 200 currencies.
- **Recurring Billing:** Automation for long-term clients.
- **Mobile Apps:** Full-featured iOS and Android apps.
- **Integrated Payments:** Accept Credit Cards, ACH, and Apple Pay directly from invoices.

## 💰 Pricing & Business Model
- **Starter:** $0/mo (Unlimited invoicing and bookkeeping).
- **Pro:** $16/mo (Auto-import transactions, receipt scanning).
- **Transaction Fees:** 
    - Credit Cards: 2.9% + $0.60.
    - AMEX: 3.4% + $0.60.
    - ACH: 1% ($1 min).

## 💻 Developer Experience
- **API:** **GraphQL** API (`https://gql.waveapps.com/graphql/public`).
- **Auth:** OAuth 2.0.
- **Tools:** Interactive GraphQL Explorer for testing queries.
- **Capabilities:** Create invoices, sync customers, access accounting data.

## 📝 Notes
- Strong "free" value proposition makes it the standard for freelancers.
- Use of GraphQL is a modern choice that developers appreciate.
- Revenue primarily comes from payment processing and payroll add-ons.
