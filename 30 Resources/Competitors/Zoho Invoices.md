# Zoho Invoices

## 📖 Definition
A comprehensive invoicing solution from the Zoho ecosystem, currently offered as a **forever-free** product for small businesses.

## 🛠️ Key Features
- **Quote-to-Cash:** Estimates, tax-compliant invoices, and recurring billing.
- **Payment Collection:** Integrates with Stripe, PayPal, and Square.
- **Expense & Time Tracking:** Log expenses and billable hours directly into invoices.
- **Client Portal:** Dedicated space for clients to manage their payments and history.
- **Mobile/Desktop Apps:** Available across all major platforms (iOS, Android, Windows, macOS).

## 💰 Pricing & Business Model
- **Zoho Invoice:** **Free** (No subscription fees).
- **Upgrade Path:** Upsells to **Zoho Billing** for advanced subscription and usage-based billing features.
- **API Limits:** Free plan capped at 1,000 requests per day.

## 💻 Developer Experience
- **API:** REST API (v3) using JSON.
- **Auth:** OAuth 2.0 with Regional Endpoints (US, EU, IN, etc.).
- **SDKs:** Official support for PHP, Python, Java, and Node.js.
- **Webhooks:** Support for real-time automation.

## 🔗 Connections
- **Projects:** [[BlackBills]]

## 📝 Notes
- "Forever-free" model is a massive loss-leader to pull users into the Zoho ecosystem.
- Regional endpoints can be a slight friction point for developers building global integrations.
- Rate limiting on the free plan is a significant constraint for high-volume users.
