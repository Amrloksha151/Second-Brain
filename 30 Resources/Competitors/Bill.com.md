# BILL (formerly Bill.com)

## 📖 Definition
A financial operations platform that automates accounts payable (AP) and accounts receivable (AR) for businesses. It focuses on end-to-end automation of the bill-to-pay process.

## 🛠️ Key Features
- **Accounts Payable (AP):** AI-powered invoice entry, approval workflows, ACH/check/wire payments.
- **Accounts Receivable (AR):** Custom invoices, automated reminders, and online payment portals.
- **Spend & Expense:** Integrated corporate cards and budget tracking.
- **Integrations:** Syncs with QuickBooks, Xero, Sage Intacct, NetSuite, and Microsoft Dynamics.

## 💰 Pricing & Business Model
- **Essentials:** ~$45/user/mo (Basic AP/AR).
- **Team:** ~$55-65/user/mo (QuickBooks/Xero sync).
- **Corporate:** ~$79-89/user/mo (**API Access**, custom policies).
- **Enterprise:** Custom (ERP integrations, SSO).
- **Transaction Fees:** ACH (~$0.59), Checks (~$1.69).

## 💻 Developer Experience
- **API:** RESTful (v3 recommended).
- **Access:** Requires **Corporate** or **Enterprise** plan for production.
- **Sandbox:** Free developer sandbox at developer.bill.com.
- **Authentication:** `devKey` + `sessionId`.
